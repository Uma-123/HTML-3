Our Mission:

Our Values: Honesty, Risk-Taking, Making Things the Best They Can Possibly Be.

Our Purpose: We make innovative products coupled with quick and easy services that help make people’s lives simple and happy.

Our Goal: Become a competitor in the Glasses Market competing with top companies like Ray-ban and Oakley by the year 2020.

Vivid Description: We will build a glasses manufacturer / retailer that will shape the glasses Market & World. Through our ingenuity, we will create customization and simplicity – we will make it so the lowly worker with facial distortions has fitted glasses putting them on the same level as their peers. We will develop new ways to find glasses if they’re lost – making the customer pay less and live better. Xino Sunglasses – will make the world a happier and simpler place to live in.
